Legal & Privacy

Last Updated: March 2026

The Office of Tyler Nicholas ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information through this public service portal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal data to ensure effective communication and functionality of this platform. The data we collect includes:

  • Information you provide directly: When you use our Secure Inquiry Form or "Let's Talk" feature, we collect your full legal name, email address, subject of inquiry, and the contents of your message.
  • Automatically collected data: We may collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system strictly for security and anti-spam purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for public service and official correspondence. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, concerns, or collaboration proposals.
  • Maintain a secure internal registry of community engagement for the Office.
  • Improve the functionality and security of our digital infrastructure.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage and Security

All inquiries and personal data submitted through this website are transmitted securely and stored in an encrypted database utilizing industry-standard infrastructure (Supabase). Access to this data is strictly limited to authorized personnel within the Office of Tyler Nicholas.

4. Cookie Policy

This website utilizes cookies—small text files placed on your device—to enhance your browsing experience. Our use of cookies is strictly limited to:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, enabling core features like security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Session Management: To remember your navigation state during your active visit.

We do not use intrusive third-party tracking cookies or targeted advertising cookies. By continuing to use this website, you consent to our use of essential cookies.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you retain full rights over your personal data. You may request to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion or removal of your correspondence from our official registry.

6. Contact Us
