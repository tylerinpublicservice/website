Official Statement

A Commitment to Serve

Public service begins with a simple belief: that every person has a role to play in building a better country.

As young people, we cannot wait for the future to be handed to us. We must be willing to step forward, serve our communities, contribute our ideas, and take responsibility for the Seychelles we want to inherit. Our voices matter, our work matters, and our generation must be ready to participate not only as observers, but as leaders, builders, volunteers, and citizens committed to national progress.

The Office of Tyler Nicholas is founded on that commitment. It exists to serve, to listen, to support youth, to encourage civic action, and to help create a stronger connection between young people and the future of our nation. We are here to work for better opportunities, stronger communities, and a society where every young person feels seen, respected, and empowered to contribute.

To the adults and leaders of today, youth leadership should not be treated as something for tomorrow. Young people are ready now. They have the energy, creativity, courage, and vision needed to help solve real challenges. When youth are given space, guidance, trust, and responsibility, they do not weaken leadership. They strengthen it.

Our country’s future depends on all generations working together. It depends on young people stepping up, adults opening doors, communities supporting one another, and every citizen choosing service over silence. A brighter Seychelles will not be built by one person, one office, or one generation alone. It will be built by all of us.

We are here to serve. We are here to volunteer. We are here to work for the better. We are here to help build a Seychelles where progress is shared, leadership is inclusive, and the future belongs to everyone.

Tyler P. Nicholas
