About The Honorable Member

A dedicated voice for the next generation of Seychellois leadership.

Tyler Nicholas is a young public service leader dedicated to youth empowerment and national progress in Seychelles. As an Honorable Member Elect of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly, he represents the voice and interests of young people while contributing to vital civic dialogue and structural development.

His work is grounded in the belief that true national development requires the active participation of informed, equipped, and empowered youth. He focuses intensely on creating meaningful opportunities, promoting civic responsibility, and fostering leadership frameworks that will sustain the Republic for decades to come.
