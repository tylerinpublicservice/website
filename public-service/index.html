The Calling

The Vast World of Public Service

Public service is not confined to a title or an office. It is a shared responsibility to uplift our communities, protect the vulnerable, and build frameworks for progress. Inspiring others to enter public service is essential because the true strength of a nation relies entirely on the collective willpower and dedication of its citizens.

Youth Concerns Portal

Public service requires listening. This portal allows young citizens of Seychelles to submit concerns, policy ideas, or community issues directly to the Office.

Submissions are reviewed by our team and, where appropriate, structured into formal motions or community action plans.
