Professional Background

Meet Tyler Nicholas

Tyler P. Nicholas is a dedicated Seychellois youth leader, civic advocate, and public servant committed to advancing national progress through community engagement and policy advocacy.

Recognizing that the future of Seychelles relies on the active participation of its younger generations, Tyler has structured his professional and civic journey around empowering youth, bridging the gap between citizens and leadership, and fostering tangible community development.

As an Honorable Member of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly’s 21st Cohort, he serves as a direct voice for the youth, bringing critical community concerns to the national stage. His role within the Assembly focuses on shaping progressive, youth-centric policies that address education, participation, and societal well-being.

On the international front, Tyler serves as a Youth Ambassador for a U.S. Department of State program, participating in global leadership exchanges. This role allows him to bridge local Seychellois initiatives with international civic standards, bringing global perspectives back home to strengthen local community frameworks.

Beyond policy and diplomacy, Tyler is the Founder & Chairman of the Board of the Tyler Nicholas Foundation. The Foundation operates as the philanthropic arm of his public service mission, directly executing community support projects, educational initiatives, and grassroots volunteer efforts to uplift those who need it most.

At his core, Tyler remains a volunteer—driven by the belief that true leadership is rooted in service, and that building a stronger, more united Seychelles requires the commitment of all its citizens.

Mission of the Office

The Office of Tyler Nicholas operates as a structured platform designed to organize, amplify, and execute public service initiatives. It is built on the foundation of transparency, accountability, and direct action.

By centralizing advocacy efforts, the Office ensures that youth representation is not just symbolic, but results-driven. We aim to connect young citizens with the resources, leadership opportunities, and platforms they need to influence the future of Seychelles.

Core Areas of Focus

  • Youth Advocacy

    Ensuring young voices influence national policy and civic discourse.

  • Civic Engagement

    Encouraging active volunteerism and participation in democratic processes.

  • Community Action

    Direct philanthropic support and project execution through the Foundation.
